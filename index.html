What is Talk Club?

In its simplest form Talk Club is a talking and listening movement helping people get mentally fit. They provide a support network and community by hosting talking and listening clubs throughout the country and across the world. Many people don’t find it easy to talk about their feelings, to many it’s a foreign language they don’t know how to speak. So the question is simple - it starts with “How are you? Out of 10?” and goes from there.

Founded in 2019, Talk Club has grown from a small group of men in Bristol to a global community of over 3000 people and 70 groups, with notable figures like Liam Gallagher and Tyson Fury getting involved and supporting the movement.

Have a watch of Ben's TED talk on how it all began, the reason behind it and the journey so far. And to find out more about what the Talk Club team are up to and how to get involved, head over to their website.

The BBF + Talk Club story

We first came together when Ben Akers & Gavin Thorpe (founders of Talk Club) approached us looking for space to host the first ever Talk Club meeting. He wanted an environment where men would feel comfortable and be able to take those all-important first steps through the door - the BBF Tap Room was ideal.

Next came Clear Head. The Talk Club team were super keen to collaborate on an alcohol-free beer and had the name in the bag, we just needed some time to work out how to make it. Thankfully lockdown gave Tristan (our head brewer) some time to create, trial, re-create and finally produce the finest alcohol-free beer we’d ever tasted!

Since we began collaborating with Talk Club, the donations from the sale of Clear Head has helped the team take on permanent offices at the Tobacco Factory over the road as well as recruiting their first full-time employee. And the growth doesn’t stop there, BBF’s input to date has hit £123k and counting!

Long may this collaboration continue, we can’t wait to see what they do next.
